Definition of done

MVP is done when
A real person can visit the deployed URL, sign in with Google, upload a CSV or PDF bank statement from a Norwegian bank, see their transactions automatically categorized, correct any wrong categories in two taps, and view a visual summary of where their money went that month.
Explicitly out of scope for MVP

Subscriptions detection, savings goals, debt tracking, loan contracts, AI insights, forecasting, sole proprietor mode, multi-account support, bank integrations, mobile app.

The one question MVP must answer

Will a real Norwegian user trust this app enough to upload their bank statement and find the result useful?
